    Welcome to the Registration Numbers App!x

    Let me introduce you to some of the basic features available to use:

    * You can add a registration number plate by entering a valid registration number

    * Valid registration numbers are in the format of: 2 alphabet characters, followed by a space, and 3-5 numbers afterwards

    * There are notifications for any user mistakes or errors you may make

    * You have the ability to filter results by area using the drop down menu

    * After filtering click the Show All button to show all registration numbers

    * Registration numbers may be deleted individually by hovering over the individual number and clicking the close button

    * The app supports local storage. The registration numbers are automatically saved and available upon refresh

    * There is a limit imposed of 15 registration numbers that can be added at a time to accomodate the space available to display them
